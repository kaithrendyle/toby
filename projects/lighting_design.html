Pendant Light

Description

Designed by Toby Lee Designs. Made in Canada. Celestial has an opaque lampshade that directs the light downwards and creates both a task and ambient light. Large lamp used to create as much light as possible from a single source. Lampshade is a large dome shaped structure with a matte finish to reflect the surface of the moon. Edges are left rough to create a more interesting shape. The inner side of the dome is painted in a reflective gold finish to emit a golden light imitating the sun. Use of a dimmable LED light creates an energy efficient, sleek and modern luminaire. Celestial is an ideal pendant light for both ambient and task lighting with stunning visual impact.
