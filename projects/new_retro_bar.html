New Retro Bar

an entertainment bar themed around a 60's style diner.

the design incorporates casual style seating both indoors and outdoors.

the covered patio is a main attraction, featuring a DJ booth and dance floor, seating and a VIP lounge and bar.

columns are concealed within an ice cream cone design, while booth seating is in the form of vintage cadillacs.
