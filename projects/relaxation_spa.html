Relaxation Spa

A relaxation spa located in the Rocky Mountains next to a hotel resort.

Use of natural stone and wood finishes creates a cohesive environment with the surrounding mountains.

The spa uses the natural resources from its surroundings to create a hot spring pool and cold dunk pools using filtered river water.

Features include a manicure and pedicure spa, flotation tanks, single and couple's massage/facial rooms, detox baths, oxygen bar, juice bar and outdoor yoga studio.

The large pool flows from indoors to the outside patio and is long enough for lap swimming.
