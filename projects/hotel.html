HOTEL

beach-side hotel located in Sanya Bay, China.

themes are based on the characters and setting of the movie “Grand Budapest Hotel”.

design concept features bold color schemes and use of geometric patterns.

areas designed include lobby and lounge, business center, all-day dining, specialty restaurant, all guestrooms.
